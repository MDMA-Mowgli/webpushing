MEET THE ARTIST

Jean-Baptiste Launay, known as “Jisbar”, is an internationally celebrated French pop-street artist who has collaborated with some of the world’s biggest celebrities and most iconic brands like Armani, BMW, Ducati, LG, Dj Snake, etc.

He often looks to the past to find inspiration from visionaries like Leonardo Da Vinci, Salvador Dali, Picasso, Andy Warhol and applies his unique, pop-street artistic style to create an entirely new interpretation and a one-of-a-kind piece of artwork. Jisbar’s work has been exhibited at the most prestigious galleries and museums across the world such as the Louvre and Eden Gallery in NYC.
